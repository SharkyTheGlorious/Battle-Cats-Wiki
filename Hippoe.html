HIPPOE

Hippoe is the first boss you encounter on the stage, Japan, the 8th stage of the game. This enemy isn't a peon like the other enemies, it has high

health and deals decently high damage. This enemy is also backed up by many peons, making it harder to damage this boss because the other peons take

the hits

Health points: 1,000 hp

Damage: 100 dmg
