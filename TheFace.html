THE FACE

The Face, is the first boss encountered in the game. This is the boss of Empire of Cats, chapter one. This boss has the highest health, and damage so far. It has relatively high range, making this bossfight hard. It has slow attack rate however, allowing for the player to get windows to attack. This makes the boss slightly easier. This enemy, has two knockbacks, and the stage has several Mini-Bosses and peons.
