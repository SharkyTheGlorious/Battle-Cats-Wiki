DARK EMPEROR NYANDAM

Dark Emperor Nyandam, is the second boss encountered. He is the boss of "The Emperor in Darkness", the second chapter of Empire of cats. This boss, once having all treasures, has the same health as The Face. However, at this point in the game, when you don't have all treasures, he has higher stats than The Face, making him a harder bossfight.
